Who are we?

We are a group of physicians with a common interest to create a healthier society.

What is our aim?

We aim to provide standard, unbiased, equal, equitable, evidence based health care that is devoid of any conflict of interest.

Are we any different?

Yes we are, we understand the importance of health and the negative impact of disease on an individual's life.

We also understand that the patient is not just a body of symptoms but an individual with his/her own sets of fear and worries.

We understand the importance of continuous education, skills development, research and keen observation.

We also possess the audacity to question common beliefs, thought process and status quo.
